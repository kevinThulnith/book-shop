Help Center

Admin Help

As an Admin, you have full control over the system. You can manage users, items, and view all orders.

  • User Management: Add, edit, and delete users of all types (Admin, Staff, Customer). You have full control over each user's account details.
  • Item Management: Add, edit, and delete items in the inventory. You have full control over the product catalog including pricing, descriptions, and availability.
  • Order Management: View all orders placed by customers and create new orders on behalf of any customer in the system.
  • System Administration: Access to all system settings and configurations.

Staff Help

As a Staff member, you can manage orders and inventory.

  • Customer Management: Edit customer details and assist them with their accounts.
  • Order Management: View all orders and create new orders by selecting specific customers from the system.
  • Inventory Management: Update the stock quantity and details of items in the inventory.
  • Profile Management: Update your own staff profile and account settings.

Customer Help

As a Customer, you can browse items, manage your shopping cart, and place orders.

  • Shopping: Browse all available items in the catalog with detailed information about each product.
  • Shopping Cart: Add items to your cart, adjust quantities, and proceed to checkout when ready.
  • Purchasing: Complete the checkout process to buy items in your cart using various payment methods.
  • My Orders: View your past orders, track their status, and review order history.
  • Profile Management: Edit your personal information, shipping addresses, and account settings.
